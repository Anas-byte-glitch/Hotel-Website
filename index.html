Our Story

A Century of
Timeless Elegance

Founded in 1921 by the visionary architect Édouard Aurèle, our hotel has long been the preferred address of royalty, artists, diplomats, and discerning travellers who seek more than accommodation — they seek an experience.

Every corridor, every suite, every detail has been meticulously designed to transport you into a world where time slows, beauty reigns, and service is an art form.
